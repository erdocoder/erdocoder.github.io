Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 22, 2026

ShaboTech operates the mobile application Equation Genius. Equation Genius is an educational math game with single-player and multiplayer modes, Apple Game Center integration, Apple CloudKit support for multiplayer, and anonymous usage analytics.

This Privacy Policy explains how Equation Genius handles information when you use the application. By using the app, you agree to the practices described below. ShaboTech does not sell, rent, or share personal information for advertising or marketing purposes.

Information We Do Not Collect

Equation Genius does not collect:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or postal address.
  • Precise location information.
  • Advertising identifiers used for profiling.
  • Your contacts, photos, or personal files.
  • Any game answers or equations you enter while playing.

The game is designed for educational and offline use. Personal information is never sold and data is processed only when required for core functionality.

Data Used by the App

1. Game Center

If you sign in with Apple Game Center, Apple provides a unique player identifier that enables:

  • Leaderboards
  • Achievements (when enabled)
  • Multiplayer matchmaking

Apple manages all Game Center account information. ShaboTech only receives the identifier necessary for gameplay.

2. Multiplayer

When participating in multiplayer games, the app may temporarily use:

  • Apple Game Center player identifiers
  • Match room codes
  • Room status (host/guest and connection state)
  • Apple CloudKit services required to support multiplayer functionality

Multiplayer data is processed through Apple's Game Center and CloudKit services solely to establish and maintain active game sessions. ShaboTech does not use this information for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Anonymous Usage Analytics

Equation Genius uses Firebase Analytics (Google) to collect anonymous usage statistics that help improve the application.

Analytics may include:

  • App launches
  • Button selections
  • Match starts and completions
  • Feature usage frequency
  • Device model and operating system version

This information:

  • Is anonymous and aggregated.
  • Cannot identify you personally.
  • Is not used for advertising or marketing profiling.
  • Does not include your game answers or personal content.

Firebase processes analytics data according to Google's Privacy Policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Third-Party Services

Equation Genius uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple Game Center
  • Apple CloudKit
  • Google Firebase Analytics

These services are governed by their own privacy policies.

Apple Privacy Policy:
https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Local Device Data

Equation Genius stores basic game data locally on your device, including:

  • Game settings and preferences.
  • Scores and gameplay progress.

Some multiplayer-related information may be processed through Apple's CloudKit services to support online gameplay. Apple manages this infrastructure according to its own privacy policies.

Cookies

The Equation Genius mobile application does not use cookies. The accompanying website may use standard browser cookies required for normal website functionality.

Security

We rely on Apple's secure services and Google's secure Firebase infrastructure. While we use industry-standard security measures, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

Equation Genius is intended for educational use and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe personal information has been submitted through external communication, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect new features or legal requirements. The latest version will always be published on this page.
