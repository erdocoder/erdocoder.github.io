Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 22, 2026

ShaboTech operates the mobile application Equation Genius. Version 1.26 provides a single-player experience with no multiplayer or in-app purchases. Version 2.0 introduces optional multiplayer features, Game Center integration, in-app purchases, and anonymous usage analytics.

This Privacy Policy explains how Equation Genius handles information when you use the app. By using the application, you agree to the practices described below. ShaboTech does not sell, rent, or share personal information for advertising or marketing purposes.

Information We Do Not Collect

Equation Genius does not collect:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or postal address
  • Precise location information
  • Advertising identifiers for profiling
  • Your contacts, photos, or personal files
  • Any game answers or equations you enter while playing

The game is designed for safe educational and offline use. Personal information is never sold and data is processed only when necessary for core functionality.

Data Used by the App

1. Game Center (Optional)

If you sign in with Apple Game Center, Apple provides a unique player identifier that allows:

  • Leaderboards
  • Achievements (if enabled)
  • Multiplayer matchmaking

Apple manages all Game Center account information. ShaboTech only receives the player identifier required for gameplay.

2. Multiplayer

When participating in multiplayer games, the app may temporarily use:

  • Apple Game Center player identifiers
  • Match room codes
  • Room status (host/guest and connection state)

This information exists only while the multiplayer session is active and is not permanently stored or exported outside Apple's services.

3. In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed entirely by Apple. ShaboTech never receives or stores your payment information. Apple may provide purchase entitlement information so the app can:

  • Unlock multiplayer features
  • Restore previous purchases on new devices

4. Anonymous Usage Analytics

Equation Genius uses Firebase Analytics (Google) to collect anonymous usage statistics that help improve the app.

Anonymous analytics may include:

  • App launches
  • Button selections
  • Match starts and completions
  • Feature usage frequency
  • Device model and operating system version

This information:

  • Is anonymous and aggregated
  • Cannot identify you personally
  • Is not used for advertising or marketing profiling
  • Does not include your game answers or personal content

Firebase processes analytics data according to Google's Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Local Device Data

Equation Genius may store game progress locally on your device, including:

  • Unlocked levels
  • Scores and streaks
  • Remaining free multiplayer matches

This information stays on your device unless you choose to back it up using your personal Apple iCloud account.

Cookies

The Equation Genius mobile application does not use cookies. If you visit the Equation Genius website, standard browser cookies may be used for normal website functionality.

Security

We rely on Apple's secure services and Google's secure Firebase infrastructure. Although we use industry-standard security measures, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

Equation Genius is designed for educational use and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe personal information has been submitted through external communication, please contact us so appropriate action can be taken.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect new features or legal requirements. The latest version will always be published on this page.
