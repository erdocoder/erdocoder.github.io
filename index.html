Equation Genius

Train your brain by solving auto-generated algebra-style equations.

Equation Genius is a fast-paced puzzle game that helps improve your mental math through progressively challenging equation solving. Every equation is randomly generated, ensuring every game feels fresh.

Solve equations using addition, subtraction, multiplication and division as the game introduces increasingly challenging equation patterns.

Play endlessly in Play Mode, master specific levels in Challenge Mode, compete against friends and players around the world in online multiplayer, or sharpen your skills against AI opponents.

Features

  • Auto-generated algebra-style equations
  • Whole-number solutions only
  • Increasing difficulty and new equation patterns
  • Play Mode and Challenge Mode
  • Online multiplayer
  • AI opponents
  • Game Center leaderboards

Available on iPhone, iPad  •  Compatible with Mac
