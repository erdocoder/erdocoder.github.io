Equation Genius

Equation Genius helps you train your brain by solving quick algebra-style math equations. Every equation is auto-generated, and all solutions are whole integers.

Solve equations using addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. The difficulty increases as you progress, introducing new equation patterns and more challenging structures.

Play Mode tests how far you can go. Challenge Mode lets you practise individual levels and improve your personal best. Multiplayer Mode allows you to compete in quick online matches, challenge friends using a room code, or practise against AI opponents.

Key Features

  • Auto-generated algebra-style equations
  • Whole-number answers with no decimals or fractions
  • Increasing difficulty and new equation types
  • Play Mode and Challenge Mode
  • Online multiplayer and friend matches
  • AI opponents for practice
  • Time-based scoring and personal bests
  • Game Center leaderboard
