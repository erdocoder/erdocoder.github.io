Equation Genius

Train your brain by solving auto-generated algebra-style equations.

Equation Genius is a puzzle game designed to improve your mental math through fast, progressively challenging equation solving. Every equation is randomly generated, ensuring that each game offers a fresh experience.

Solve equations using addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division as the difficulty gradually increases with new equation patterns and more complex challenges.

Test your speed and accuracy in Play Mode, master individual stages in Challenge Mode, or compete against players around the world in online multiplayer. You can also sharpen your skills against AI opponents whenever you like.

Features

  • Auto-generated algebra-style equations
  • Whole-number solutions only
  • Increasing difficulty and new equation types
  • Play Mode and Challenge Mode
  • Online multiplayer and friend matches
  • AI opponents
  • Game Center leaderboards
